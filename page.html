    The DJI Mavic 3 Enterprise Series has two versions: DJI Mavic 3E and DJI Mavic 3T. DJI Mavic 3E is equipped with a 20MP wide-angle camera with a 4/3 CMOS and a zoom camera to deliver powerful imaging capability frequently used for surveying, inspection, and security. DJI Mavic 3T is equipped with a 48MP wide-angle camera with a 1/2-inch CMOS, a zoom camera, and a thermal camera to deliver thermal imaging, useful for night rescues, firefighting, and power inspections.

  • Do accessories of the DJI Mavic 3 Enterprise Series support PD/QC/PPS fast charging?

    The Intelligent Flight Battery, DJI USB-C Power Adapter (100W), and DJI Mavic 3 Battery Charging Hub (100W) of the DJI Mavic 3 Enterprise Series support PD and PPS fast charging protocols. The DJI RC Pro Enterprise supports PD and QC protocols.

  • What DJI Mavic 3 Enterprise Series accessories are worth purchasing? What DJI Mavic 3 accessories can be used on DJI Mavic 3 Enterprise Series?

    A range of useful accessories have been launched with the DJI Mavic 3 Enterprise Series, including the DJI Mavic 3 Enterprise Series RTK Module, DJI Mavic 3 Enterprise Series Speaker, DJI Mavic 3 Battery Charging Hub (100W), DJI USB-C Power Adapter (100W), DJI Mavic 3 Enterprise Series Battery Kit, and DJI Mavic 3 Enterprise Series Propellers. All can be purchased separately online at the DJI Store. The DJI Mavic 3 Enterprise Series can also use Mavic 3's intelligent flight battery and propeller guards.

  • How long does it take to fully charge an Intelligent Flight Battery of the DJI Mavic 3 Enterprise Series?

    It takes the DJI USB-C Power Adapter (100W) or the DJI Mavic 3 Battery Charging Hub (100W) approximately 70 minutes to fully charge an intelligent battery, and approximately 50 minutes to charge it to 90%. The powerful fast charge design meets the need for continuous operation and increased efficiency.

  • Can the DJI Mavic 3 Enterprise Series be charged with any USB-C charger?

    Yes, but it is recommended to use the DJI USB-C Power Adapter (100W) or DJI Mavic 3 Battery Charging Hub (100W) for fast charging.
